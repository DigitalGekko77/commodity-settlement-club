The Problem

The international settlement system is broken

Every existing solution suffers from one or more critical flaws:

SWIFT / Banks

1–3% fee. 7–14 days. Dependency on USD correspondent accounts. Any deal can be blocked from Washington.

CIPS / SPFS

Alternative payment rails — but still in fiat. Yuan depends on PBOC decisions. Inflation, devaluation, political risk of issuing CB.

mBridge / CBDC

Digital versions of fiat currencies. Whoever controls emission gets seigniorage. Single issuer = single point of pressure.

Cryptocurrencies

Volatility. Coin tainting via chainalysis. Exchange-level blocking. Unsuitable for interstate settlements.

Gold tokens

Physical storage. Expensive logistics. Trust in issuer. Political vulnerability.

Direct barter

Requires matching needs at one moment. Doesn't scale. No clearing. No delivery verification.

Solution

Commodity Settlement Club

Each country issues a commodity token pegged to gold as a unit of account. The token is redeemed with real export commodity. Collateral is the country's production capacity.

No single issuer

Each country only issues its own tokens. No seigniorage, no pressure point.

Commodity-backed

Not gold reserves, not forex — production capacity. Oil, gas, metals, grain.

Automatic clearing

Smart contract finds offsetting claims and settles them. Russia → China → India → Russia — one click.

Key difference: CIPS and mBridge change the rails but not the currency. CSC changes the nature of the settlement instrument itself — from fiat debt to commodity claim.

Benefits

What the state gets

The system doesn't bypass the state — it gives it tools it never had. Every antagonist gets a role.

Central Bank

Oversight of OTP as licensed NCOs. Lombard lending against tokens. Observer with veto. USD-isolated circuit.

Finance Ministry

+$2.5–5B/year to budget at scale on Russia–India oil corridor. Automatic duty accrual at token redemption.

Customs Service

Each token = customs declaration. Auto-integration with customs systems. Real-time cargo tracking. Daily statistics.

Tax Authority

OTP operators as tax agents. Automatic VAT and income tax accrual. Unified database of all commodity transactions.

Ministry of Economy

Commodity flow analytics. Observer with veto on nomenclature. Strategic export control.

Security Services

Full visibility into strategic export flows. Every transaction recorded on blockchain. No cargo concealment.
